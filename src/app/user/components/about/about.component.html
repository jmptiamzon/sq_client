About OACC

A quiz is a form of game or mind sport, in which the players attempt to answer question correctly. It is a game to test your knowledge and to determine the traits and characteristics of the quiz taker. In addition, there are a lot of Filipinos having a hard time in choosing which course to take in college. After that, there are some students in high school that does not know which course to take in them. In other words, the students choose a course without even researching what the course do. Therefore, the student will have hard time taking the course. In addition, the students will spend years until they notice that they have chose the wrong course, resulting in waste of time, finance and effort on a wrong course. In conclusion, students are having a difficult time in choosing the appropriate course to their liking.

OACC or Online Assessment for College Courses is an application which helps students where they can possibly study and what course they can possibly take.
